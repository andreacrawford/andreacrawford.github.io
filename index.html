Hi, I'm Andrea and this is my recipe blog where I celebrate my love for plant-based food. In addition to being meat and dairy-free, most of my recipes are free processed foods and sugar. Andrea's Kitchen is a healthy vegan lifestyle blog with a focus on wholesome, simple recipes. Feel free to read my story on my About page, or if you're in a hurry, head straight for my Recipes page and get cooking!
